Forum

The best way for discussing improvement ideas or getting help is our discussion forum. Sign up and join the conversation!

Chat

We also have a development channel on IRC and a group on Telegram. The two chats are bridged, so you can join any one of them to see all conversations.

  • #librepcb on Freenode
  • LibrePCB_dev on Telegram

Note that the chat is mainly for discussing the development of LibrePCB. To get help, the forum is better suited.
